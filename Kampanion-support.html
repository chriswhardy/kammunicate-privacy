Getting Started

What is Kampanion?

Kampanion is a companion app for iPhone that allows carers and support providers to communicate remotely with someone using the Kammunicate AAC app on their iPad. Send quick-response cards, drawings, photos, and stickers to stay connected wherever you are.

Who is Kampanion for?

Kampanion is designed for carers, family members, and support providers who want to communicate remotely with someone using Kammunicate. It is the companion to the main Kammunicate iPad app.

What do I need to get started?

  • An iPhone running iOS 18 or later
  • An iCloud account signed in on your device
  • Access to the Kammunicate iPad to complete the pairing process
  • No account creation, email address, or password is needed

Pairing with Kammunicate

How do I pair with a Kammunicate iPad?

  1. On the Kammunicate iPad, open the Remote Carer settings and create a pairing invitation
  2. A QR code and a 6-character pairing code will appear on the iPad screen
  3. Open Kampanion on your iPhone and either scan the QR code or enter the code manually
  4. Once paired, you can begin sending and receiving messages

Tip: Pairing codes expire after 24 hours and can only be used once. If your code has expired, ask the person with the Kammunicate iPad to create a new invitation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Kampanion require an internet connection?

Yes. Kampanion needs an internet connection to send and receive messages with the paired Kammunicate iPad. Messages are synced through Apple's CloudKit service.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All messages are end-to-end encrypted using AES-GCM encryption. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties. The app contains zero third-party code or tracking. For full details, please see our Privacy Policy.

What can I send from Kampanion?

You can send quick-response cards (organised into categories like Carer Responses, Questions, Health & Comfort, and more), drawings, photos from your camera or photo library, and stickers.

How do I know when a new message arrives?

Kampanion sends push notifications when a new message arrives from the paired Kammunicate iPad. You can also see a green presence indicator when the other device is online.

Can I pair with more than one Kammunicate iPad?

Kampanion connects to one Kammunicate iPad at a time. To connect to a different iPad, disconnect from the current one first, then pair with the new device.

How do I disconnect from a paired device?

Open the settings in Kampanion and select disconnect. This will permanently delete all encryption keys and cached message data from your device.

How do I delete my data?

Disconnect from the paired device to delete all synced data and encryption keys. To delete all data completely, remove the app from your device.

The app seems slow or unresponsive. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your iPhone. If you continue to experience problems, please contact us with details about what you were doing when the issue occurred.

How do I update the app?

Kampanion is updated through the App Store. You can enable automatic updates in your device's Settings, or manually check for updates in the App Store.

Troubleshooting

Cannot pair with Kammunicate iPad

  • Ensure both devices have an active internet connection
  • Check that both devices are signed into iCloud
  • Verify the pairing code has not expired (codes are valid for 24 hours)
  • Try creating a new pairing invitation on the Kammunicate iPad
  • If scanning the QR code does not work, try entering the 6-character code manually

Not receiving messages

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure push notifications are enabled for Kampanion in Settings > Notifications > Kampanion
  • Verify the Kammunicate iPad is online (look for the green presence indicator)
  • Try closing and reopening the app

Camera not working for QR scanning

  • Go to Settings > Kampanion on your device
  • Ensure camera permission is enabled
  • If the permission is on but the camera still does not work, try restarting the app
  • You can always enter the pairing code manually as an alternative

Messages showing as unreadable or garbled

  • This may indicate an encryption key mismatch
  • Try disconnecting and re-pairing with the Kammunicate iPad
  • Ensure both apps are updated to the latest version
