Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I create an avatar for each player?

A: Long-press on the circular avatar button in the center toolbar. You can create letter avatars (A-Z), icon avatars (50+ symbols), or photo avatars using your camera or photo library. Each player can have their own personalized avatar.

Q: How do I set player names?

A: Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the toolbar, then scroll to "Player Names & Roles." You can customize names for both players. Default names are "Person" and "Carer."

Q: How do I switch between players?

A: Tap the Send button (paper airplane) to send your current message and switch to the other player. The active player is indicated by a blue ring around their avatar.

Using the Drawing Canvas

Q: How do I change drawing tools?

A: Tap the brush icon in the toolbar to switch between Brush and Eraser. Long-press to access additional tool options.

Q: How do I change colors?

A: Tap the rainbow color wheel button to open the color picker. Choose from preset colors or create custom colors.

Q: Can I undo mistakes?

A: Yes! Use the undo button (curved arrow) to undo your last action. You can undo multiple times. There's also a redo button to restore undone actions.

Communication Cards

Q: How do the smart card suggestions work?

A: Kammunicate uses six different machine learning systems running on your device to suggest relevant cards based on your usage patterns, time of day, recent messages, and drawings. The more you use it, the smarter it gets!

Q: How do I choose a card?

A: Tap any card in the dock at the bottom to select it. The card will appear on your canvas. You can browse more cards by tapping the grid icon.

Q: Can I create custom cards?

A: Currently, Kammunicate comes with 200+ pre-made communication cards. Custom card creation may be added in future updates.

Photos and Media

Q: How do I add photos to messages?

A: Tap the photo icon (camera) in the toolbar. You can take a new photo with your camera or select an existing photo from your library.

Q: How do I add stickers?

A: Tap the sticker icon (smiley face) to browse and add expressive stickers to your canvas.

Communication History

Q: How do I view past messages?

A: Tap the History icon (clock) to see all previous messages. You can scroll through the timeline and see who sent what and when.

Q: Can I delete old messages?

A: Yes, long-press on any message in the history to delete it.

Privacy & Data

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Absolutely! Kammunicate processes all data 100% on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. No internet connection is required. Read our full Privacy Policy.

Q: What happens to my data if I delete the app?

A: All data is permanently deleted when you remove the app from your device. Make sure to save any important communications before deleting.

Q: Is my data backed up?

A: Your Kammunicate data is included in your device's iCloud or iTunes backups if you have backup enabled in your device settings.

Professional Features

Q: What is the Professional Learning Dashboard?

A: This feature provides caregivers and professionals with insights into communication patterns, frequently used topics, and engagement metrics. Access it by tapping the chart icon in the toolbar.

Q: What are player roles?

A: You can assign roles to each player (Needs Expresser or Support Provider). This helps the app provide more relevant card suggestions for each person's role.

Accessibility Features

Q: What is Easy Read mode?

A: Easy Read mode simplifies the interface with larger buttons and clearer labels. Enable it in Settings → Accessibility.

Q: Does Kammunicate work with VoiceOver?

A: Kammunicate includes accessibility labels for screen readers. We continuously work to improve accessibility features.

Technical Issues

Q: The app isn't responding. What should I do?

A: Try these steps in order:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • If problems persist, contact support (see below)

Q: I lost my unsaved work. Can I recover it?

A: Kammunicate automatically saves drafts every 5 seconds while you're drawing. If the app closes unexpectedly, your draft should be restored when you reopen it.

Q: What iOS version do I need?

A: Kammunicate requires iOS 15.0 or later to take advantage of the latest machine learning and accessibility features.
